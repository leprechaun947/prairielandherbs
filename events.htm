Used for making yarn since ancient times, the drop spindle is still popular today among crafters. Inexpensive and easily portable, you can spin your own yarn almost anywhere.

Learn to Spin
with a Drop Spindle

Saturday, 6th January
1:00 – 3:00 pm.

In this class we will talk about wool, its various preparations, and how to buy it. You will learn how to spin roving into yarn using a drop spindle. We will provide a drop spindle, which you get to keep, and several kinds of roving. Come with a spirit of adventure and a sense of humor; this is a tricky skill and it does take time, but I know you can do it!

Per person: $45.00
(all supplies included).

Sample Sunday
